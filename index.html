Yucheng Shi - 史淯城

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student at the University of Georgia, under the mentorship of Prof. Ninghao Liu. My research lies at the critical intersection of Data-centric AI and Responsible AI. My work focuses on improving input data quality, at training/inference phases, to boost the performance, interpretability, and safety of models. Currently, I am exploring methods to generate synthetic data for large foundation models to enhance their performance and reliability. My specific areas of research include:

  • Synthetic Data: Zero-shot image purification(ZIP), ENGAGE, ChatGraph, GiGaMAE.
  • Retrieval Augmented Generation: Retrieval Augmented Editing, Medical RAG.
  • Explainability and its utilization: XAI Usage with LLMs, Explanation of Deep Visual Neurons, XAI with Time Series.
  • Medical Foundation Models: Radiology Llama 70B Post-training (tech report).
