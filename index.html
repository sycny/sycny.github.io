Current Research

In summer 2026 I am leading a research program on self-evolving terminal agents, working with Zhongzhi Li, Ruhan Wang, Zongxia Li, and Junyao Yang. We study how an agent training system can improve along three coupled axes: the environments it trains on, the harness through which it acts, and the learning signals extracted from long trajectories. Notes and artifacts are released as the work matures.

Harness Handbook
A behavior-first map that helps people and coding agents locate where a harness behavior lives before editing it.
Agent systems · Ruhan Wang · Jun 2026
Long-Horizon Terminal Bench
Forty-six containerized tasks across 21 domains, with deterministic subtask grading and meaningful partial credit.
Benchmark · Zongxia Li · paper forthcoming
Dive into Staleness Analysis in Asynchronous RL
A mathematical and empirical guide to rollout–training mismatch, with a staleness-adaptive trust region for controlling it.
Agentic RL · Junyao Yang · note forthcoming
Continual Task Synthesis for Terminal Agents
Turning seed environments, failed trajectories, and recovery traces into SFT- and RL-ready terminal tasks.
Data engine · Zhongzhi Li · in progress
